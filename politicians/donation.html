50 Children in School

From Street to Classroom

Thanks to your donations, 50 children from Kathmandu's slums are now attending school regularly. They receive uniforms, books, and a daily nutritious meal. Priya, 8 years old, now dreams of becoming a doctor. "I love going to school," she says with a bright smile.

30 Elderly Supported

Golden Years with Dignity

Our old age home in Pokhara now provides medical care, social activities, and companionship for 30 elderly residents. Ram Bahadur, 78, says, "I have friends here and the care is wonderful. I feel like I have a family again."
